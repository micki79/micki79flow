Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Micki79 ViralFlow ("we", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our video automation service.

2. Data We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account, we access:

  • Basic profile information (username, display name) via user.info.basic scope
  • Video publishing permissions via video.publish scope

We do NOT collect: passwords, payment information, private messages, or follower lists.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To publish AI-generated video content to your connected TikTok account
  • To display your account status in our dashboard
  • To manage upload schedules and track publishing success

4. Data Storage

Your OAuth tokens are stored securely on our private server. We do not share your data with third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only while your account is connected. You can disconnect at any time, and we will delete your stored tokens.

6. Your Rights

You can request data deletion by contacting us. You can revoke access at any time through TikTok's account settings.

7. TikTok API

Our use of TikTok data complies with the TikTok Platform Developer Terms. We only access data necessary for video publishing.
